Section B Short answer

Q7
∠R = 80°, ∠S = 60°. Co-interior angles on each leg are supplementary.
Q8
∠A = ∠C = 110°. 360 − 100 − 40 = 220, shared equally between the equal pair.
Q9
Any two of: two pairs of adjacent equal sides; diagonals perpendicular; main diagonal bisects the other and bisects two corner angles; one pair of opposite angles equal.

Section C Long answer

Q10
∠C = 70°, ∠D = 105°. AB ∥ DC ⇒ ∠A + ∠D = 180° and ∠B + ∠C = 180°.
Q11
70°, 110°, 110°. Base angles equal (the other base angle is 70°); each top angle = 180 − 70 = 110°.
Q12
(i) Square. (ii) Yes — a parallelogram has a pair of parallel sides, so it fits the trapezium definition. (iii) No — a kite has only two pairs of adjacent equal sides, not all four.
