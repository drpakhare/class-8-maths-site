Section A Objective

Q1
(a) 2⁸. Same base, add exponents: 3 + 5 = 8.
Q2
(b) 5⁶. Power of a power: multiply 2 × 3 = 6.
Q3
5. Same base, subtract: 9 − 4 = 5.
Q4
(a) 20³. Power of a product: 4³ × 5³ = (4 × 5)³.
Q5
True. This is the power-of-a-power law.
Q6
(a) Both true, R explains A. (5²)³ = 5²ˣ³ = 5⁶.
