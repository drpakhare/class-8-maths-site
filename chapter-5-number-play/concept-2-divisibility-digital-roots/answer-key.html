Section C Long answer

Q10
Divisible by 3 only. Digit sum 30 → divisible by 3, not 9; ends in 9 → not 2, 4, 5, 6, 10; alternating sum 4 → not 11.
Q11
Digital root = 2. 63p and 18q are multiples of 9 (so contribute 0); 29 leaves remainder 2 on division by 9, giving digital root 2 whatever p and q are.
