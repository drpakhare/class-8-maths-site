Section B Short answer

Q7
Correct: 15p² − 6pq. The −3p must be multiplied, not added.
Q8
Correct: 5x + 10. The constants are −2 + 12 = 10, not 3.
Q9
Correct: 5a³ + 6a²b + 6ab². 6a²b and 6ab² are unlike terms and cannot be combined.

Section C Long answer

Q10
Correct: 25m² + 60mn + 36n². The middle term 2 × 5m × 6n = 60mn was omitted.
Q11
x² + 7x + 10. x×x + x×5 + 2×x + 2×5 = x² + 5x + 2x + 10.
Q12
Correct: 3y + 4. y + 2(y + 2) means y + 2y + 4 (the 2 multiplies only the bracket); it is not (y + 2)².
